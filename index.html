Privacy Policy – Galaxy Wars: Attack on Earth

Last updated: July 01, 2025

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information on our servers. All gameplay data is stored locally on the user's device. This includes:

  • Age confirmation (to verify if the user is 16 years or older)
  • Language preference
  • Game progression (unlocked levels)

2. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to serve in-game advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information to personalize ads, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising ID
  • Usage data

These data are handled by Google according to their own Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings or through the in-game options.

3. Children’s Privacy

This game is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect or store any personal information from children under the age of 13. All gameplay data — such as age confirmation, language preferences, and unlocked levels — is stored locally on the user’s device and is never transmitted or stored externally.

4. Data Security

We do not transmit gameplay data to external servers. All information is stored securely on the user's own device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
